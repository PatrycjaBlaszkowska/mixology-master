What cocktail spec means?

A cocktail spec, short for specification, is a detailed and precise recipe for making a specific cocktail.
It includes the exact measurements of each ingredient, the specific type and brand of alcohol or mixer to use,
the method of preparation, and any garnishes or finishing touches to ensure consistency in the drink's flavor and presentation.

Is bartender kit needed to make cocktails?

It is not required to have a bartender kit to make cocktails, but having one can make the process easier and more efficient.
A basic bartender kit typically includes essential tools such as a shaker, jigger, strainer, muddler, and bar spoon.
These tools can help you measure ingredients accurately, mix drinks properly, and strain out any unwanted particles.
